What is the Among Us Soundboard?

This Among Us Soundboard is a soundboard I made for a review game for my 3rd-5th grade students. Students would complete "tasks" on separate computers. However, when a student wanted to call an Emergency Meeting or report a dead body, they would push the appropriate button on the classroom TV.

I added extra buttons for more engagement in the classroom.

What's in the future of the Among Us Soundboard?

I plan to expand on this soundboard by adding more features, such as the ability for teachers to input their own "tasks."

I want to also include a feature for different computers to log into the main game to receive these tasks and display when a meeting has been called. I have noticed that when one student calls a meeting, some students will continue the task, which prolongs the meetings and gives for distraction. Adding a meeting display would allow for swifter gameplay, closer to how the mobile game functions.
